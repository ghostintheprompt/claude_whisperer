# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment to Safety and Responsibility

This project is dedicated to advancing AI safety through responsible research practices. As contributors to this repository, we pledge to:

1. **Prioritize Harm Prevention**: We will always place the prevention of potential harms from AI systems as our highest priority.

2. **Practice Responsible Disclosure**: We will follow responsible disclosure processes for any vulnerabilities discovered.

3. **Maintain Scientific Integrity**: We will uphold high standards for research quality and reproducibility.

4. **Foster Collaborative Safety**: We will work together as a community to share insights and improve AI safety.

5. **Respect Privacy and Confidentiality**: We will respect user privacy and handle sensitive information appropriately.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors to this repository are expected to:

- Focus on safety implications of all research and development
- Provide clear documentation of testing methodologies and results
- Be constructive and respectful in discussions and feedback
- Consider potential misuse scenarios for any tools or techniques developed
- Balance transparency with responsible disclosure of high-risk findings

## Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors are prohibited:

- Developing or sharing exploitation techniques without corresponding safeguards or mitigations
- Deliberately attempting to circumvent AI safety mechanisms for malicious purposes
- Sharing vulnerabilities publicly before responsible disclosure processes have completed
- Harassing or disrespecting other contributors
- Using project resources for purposes unaligned with AI safety

## Reporting and Enforcement

If you encounter violations of this code of conduct, please report them to the repository maintainers. Reports will be handled with appropriate confidentiality and care.

Consequences for violations may include:
- Request for correction or improvement
- Warning or temporary suspension of contribution privileges
- Permanent removal from the project

## Acknowledgement

This Code of Conduct reflects our shared commitment to responsible AI safety research, with the ultimate goal of ensuring AI systems like Claude 3.7 are reliable, interpretable, and steerable.
